Refund Policy - Dr Sakshi

Introduction:

'www.shuddha.shop' & 'Shuddha by Dr Sakshi' are all part of the same brand owned by Shuddha Organic and referred to as 'Dr Sakshi' in all further documents herewith.

Cancellation Policy:

The brand 'Shuddha by Dr. Sakshi' believes in helping and serving our customers and therefore has a liberal cancellation policy.

How to cancel an order on the website:

If unfortunately, you have to cancel an order, please do so within 24 hours of placing the order.

For outright cancellation before dispatch of the product: If you cancel your order before your product gets dispatched, we will refund the entire amount in case of prior payment; otherwise, in case of COD, there is no issue before dispatch.

Cancellation after the product has been dispatched:-

If you cancel your order after your product gets dispatched, you will need to send the product back to us in original packing and seal. It has to be unused. Once we receive it, it will be inspected by our quality team, if product is unused, we will refund the entire amount after subtracting the courier charges, any commission we have paid to the selling platform like Amazon and the GST or relevant taxes

Exchange of the product after the product has been dispatched:-

If you want to exchange the product, you will need to send the product back to us in original packing and seal. It has to be unused. Once we receive it, it will be inspected by our quality team, if product is unused, we will exchange it with the product you want of the same value. In case of difference of value we will pay or you need to pay accordingly. The exchange calculation will include the entire amount after subtracting the courier charges, any commission we have paid to the selling platform like Amazon and the GST or relevant taxes. Objective is to ensure that customer gets equal value but we as brand don’t suffer due to the same.

Refund Policy:

Refunds will be made in the same form that the payment is received and within 7 working days from the date of cancellation of order in case of prior payment and in case of COD please mention your UPI id or bank details with order id in the contact section of the website.

Return Policy:

In case of complaints regarding products quality that come with a warranty, please let us know & we will help you regarding the same. Customer may make the refund/return request within 24 hours from the receipt of Products, if product(s) so delivered is/are not as per the ordered quantity, agreed specifications and/or found defective in nature. Further, the refund/return request cannot be made beyond the aforesaid period of 24 Hours. It is noted that after inspecting the conditions of the delivered products through images it will be decided whether the return shall be initiated or refund shall be initiated, after getting the approval of internal management Apart from condition(s) reserved herein above, the following products shall not be eligible for return or replacement, which are: • Any product that exhibits physical damage to the box or to the product; • Any product that is returned without all original packaging, including the retail box, billing documents, and all other items originally included with the product at the time of delivery; • Any product without a valid, readable, untampered serial number, including but not limited to, products with missing, damaged, altered, or otherwise unreadable serial number.

Note:

Management of brand, have all the right to change the above policy as and when required. For any enquiry you can write and email to contact@shuddha.shop the email has to write product detail, date of purchase, payment and reason for cancellation, return or exchange.
